Primary betting trigger
Temporal weighting: 2025-26 season games (55%), 2024-25 (30%), 2023-24 (15%). Later-season games carry higher within-season weights as KenPom ratings stabilize. Recency signals (Mom, Con, ConR) sourced from Haslametrics time-dependent ratings.
